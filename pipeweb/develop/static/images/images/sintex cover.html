PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Sintex water storage solutions ensure highest standards of hygiene at a reasonable cost. The Sintex Ace, an innovation that offers side manhole feature for easy cleaning along with Sintex Pure, Sintex Titus, Reno, Loft Tanks, Sumps, FRP,  PUF and SMC panel tanks cover the entire range of water storage solutions. Sintex Plastics Division was started in the year 1975, and today we have the most diversified manufacturing capabilities in plastic processing in the world.
